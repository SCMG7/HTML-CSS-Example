Sunderland's Tournaments

A small team from University of Funderland with a combination of experts of Hardware and Software engineers are creating this tournament with an act to find the best of which are about to represent our city.
The three best teams or solo players will earn sum of prize money, only the best one will represent the City.

Need to Know

The Tournament will take place in Susnderland City Hall at 27of October 2018.

Start time 11:00 AM till late.

For more details about the location please see the map.

The games which will take role at the Tournament are:

  • League of Legends (teams)
  • DOTA II (teams)
  • Street Fighter IV (solo)
  • Fortnite (solo-duo-squad)
  • Overwatch (team)
  • Call Of Duty: Black Ops IV (team of 4-team of 6)
  • Counter Strike (teams)
